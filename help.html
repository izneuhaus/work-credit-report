Work Credit Expectations

Barring any exceptions, the expectations for work credit are as follows:
  • Tenants are required to complete 8 House Hours and 8 Collective Hours a month. An additional House Hour is required for each free-roaming pet.
  • Tenants are also required to complete 18 Maintenance Hours in a year.
  • Boarders are required to complete 4 House Hours a month.

How to read this report

This report lists all current RCHC members alongside the number of hours each currently has "banked" over the requirement for the current month (or year, in the case of Maintenance Hours).

Hour counts in the negative are highlighted in red and counts at or above the requirement are highlighted in blue.
